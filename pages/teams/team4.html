EXCELARATORS

Excelerators CET is the official go-kart and e-bike team of CET

The team has actively participated in a variety of SAE events and has earned many laurels. AIR-2 in FKDC online Go-karting competition , AIR-10 in IKC virtuals are few of the achievements which the team had consecutively procured in the last 2 years .

The team achieved an overall rank of AIR-8 and AIR-1 in the endurance category of the SAE Electric Two Wheeler Design Competition 2022 . With our past work experience and necessary exposure , we strive to advance our manufacturing process in the field of E-bikes and Go-karts and come up with upgraded yet innovative versions of the same.
