ZENITH MOTORSPORT

Official Formula Student Team of College of Engineering, Trivandrum.

Zenith Motorsport CET is the official formula student team of College of Engineering, Trivandrum. It is our vision to seek into the deepest of dedication and skill sets, inspiring the nation to get into the art and field of Formula student racing, and to craft prominent engineers using the best of technologies.

We compete in numerous national student competitions like Formula Bharat and Supra Sae. SUPRA is a national-level engineering design competition, where teams from various engineering colleges across the country participate by designing and fabricating a formula-style vehicle based on the rules and design standards of SAE international. Last year, amidst COVID-19 pandemic, we won All India rank 22 in Formula Bharath virtuals. Participation in these competitions ensure that our team members become industry ready graduates while undertaking the tasks of designing, manufacturing and testing our car.

We also endeavor to promote the culture of motorsport within our college and hope to inspire others. Our club has been inculcating passion for automobile technology by organizing various programs in our college like talk series, workshops , etc.. These sessions aim to furnish deeper understanding and ideas on automobile practices.

This year we are highly motivated to build our first car and establish our team. SAE SUPRA is the stage to showcase our potential and gain national recognition. The event will be conducted during the month of August at Buddh International Circuit, Noida whereas FORMULA BHARAT will be conducted in January 2023 at Kari Motor Speedway, Coimbatore. Besides taking our college to new heights, this will inspire the student engineers of CET, thus enriching our legacy.
